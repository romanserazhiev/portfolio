Support Engineer at K15t

I joined K15t as Support and QA Engineer in 2015 to help the fast-growing Atlassian Marketplace vendor with rapidly increased amount of customer queries while also contributing my QA sills acquired from previous experiences.

After company’s transition into a teal organization, I was overviewing Support across the company, while being responsible Documentation within my team.

Achievements
  • even though the amount of support queries the company receives per month have increase more than 2,5 times since 2015 (compared with 2018), the same 24 business hours response SLA is still maintained and not changed;
  • extended existing automations and macros for the Support System, which allowed agents to reply to common queries with one click;
  • documented all processes and how-tos around the Support Process;
  • project Lead for Hipchat to Stride migration within the company (≈50 employees). Initiated the migration, announcement and morning talks for colleagues, scheduling the backup, coordinating the switch to Stride with Atlassian.
Notable experiences
